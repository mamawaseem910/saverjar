Thank you for trusting SaverJar ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your personal privacy by keeping your financial goals, custom jar metrics, and transaction ledgers locked entirely to your local system without any network vulnerabilities.
1

Information Collection & Use

The App operates entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, upload, or sell any personal or financial information. All data you input is sandboxed safely:

  • Financial Parameters: Savings goals, custom target deadlines, container adjustments, and virtual jar allocations are strictly local.
  • Transaction Logs: Balance edits, log archives, and historical logs exist only on your physical memory using an encrypted local SQLite database.
2

Third-Party Services

We believe your budget calculations are strictly personal. To maintain ultimate device isolation:

  • The App does not utilize any third-party analytics SDKs, user behavior trackers, or cookies.
  • No advertising frameworks are integrated, ensuring zero data brokerage or background marketing profiling.
3

Device Permissions

To deliver high-fidelity tactile feedback and sensory sound cues safely offline, the App requests minimal native system capabilities:

  • Haptic Triggers: Used solely to provide sensory confirmation clicks when physical inputs or transaction increments are entered.
  • Media Playback Settings: Utilized locally to play interactive sound effects (e.g., jar drop chimes) without initiating active network connections.

SaverJar requests zero network access capabilities or private sensor logs (such as GPS, camera, or contact books) for its core operating routines.

4

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since your financial parameters are never backed up or stored on remote servers, any subsequent policy adjustments will be communicated locally via system application store updates.
